Welcome to Saipan!

Saipan is the primary destination of the Northern Mariana Islands which is a U.S. territory located in the Pacific just three hours away by plane from Japan and north of Australia. Saipan is a popular tourist destination home to beautiful beaches and world-class resorts and attractions. Saipan also has a ton of history from the Battle of Saipan which occurred during the later stages of World War II. Hafa-Adai and enjoy your stay on Saipan!

Must see Attractions

Bird Island

A well known tourist attraction on the island of Saipan, Bird Island Sanctuary is home to thousands of birds and other marine species. A short walk down stairs from the parking area, you’ll find yourself standing on a high cliff overlooking the vast blue ocean and Bird Island. There are also additional paths to explore where you can see Bird Island from ground level for a truly breathtaking experience.

The Grotto

The Grotto is a stunning and impressive natural underwater limestone cavern that reaches some 70 feet below the sea at its deepest point. Considered one of the best cavern-dive spots in the world, its wealth of underwater life—including turtles, parrotfish, many hard and soft corals and colorful nudibranchs—makes it hugely popular with snorkelers and swimmers, too.

Managaha Island

This coral islet sitting in Saipan's lagoon is a mini-paradise with a fascinating history and amazing views. Snorkelling, scuba diving and sightseeing are all popular here. Just don't forget to pack some sunscreen!

Restaurants

Himawari

Himawari offers the best fresh fish and Japanese food on Saipan. Himawari also contains a large grocery shop for baked goods and delicious bento boxes to satisfy any of your cravings.You won't be disappointed.

The Shack Saipan

The Shack Saipan is a local favourite on island providing a unique combination of meals that are truly healthy and high quality. The Shack offers Specialty Crepes (savory and sweet), Daily Plate Specials, Salads, Quinoa Boxes, Smoothies, Breakfast Bowls, Cold Beverages, Espresso Drinks, Truly Healthy Desserts and much more!

360 Revolving Restaurant

360 Revolving Restaurant is the one and only revolving restaurant in the south pacific. Great Views, Certified Angus Beef(R) Steaks, American and international cuisine, terrific drinks at reasonable prices. Lunch or Dinner, the menu has something for everyone. Enjoy tantalizing appetizers and mouth watering entrees that are perfect for any occasion.
